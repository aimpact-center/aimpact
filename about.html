About

The AImpact Center dedicates itself to exploring the effects of artificial intelligence and digital technologies on society. We aim to understand and address the ethical, social, and economic implications of these technologies, ensuring they benefit all members of society. By fostering collaboration across disciplines, we strive to create a future where AI and digital innovations enhance human well-being and promote equitable progress.

Our Goals

  • Investigate how AI and digital technologies affect various aspects of society, including economic, social, and cultural dimensions.
  • Provide evidence-based insights to inform policymakers and stakeholders about the best practices for regulating and governing AI and digital technologies.
  • Develop frameworks and guidelines to ensure that AI technologies are designed and implemented ethically.

Scientific Approach

The Center will leverage a variety of methods as represented by the diverse disciplinary commitments of its members, including but not limited to:

  • Randomized experiments to test hypotheses and establish causal relationships.
  • Mathematical foundations of emergent capabilities and cross-domain transfer of AI models, upon which to build economic and social theory.
  • Classical econometric methods such as regression analysis, instrumental variables, and difference-in-differences to analyze observational data.
  • AI-based econometrics and high-dimensional analyses of observational data to address the challenges posed by large and complex data sets.
